AI Internship - Techolution

Job Title : AI Intern
Company : Techolution
Location : Kolkata
Job Type : Internship (3 months)

Job Description
Techolution is excited to present a rewarding opportunity for an AI Intern to join our team for a 3-month internship. As an AI Intern, you will be immersed in cutting-edge projects, gaining hands-on experience in artificial intelligence within a dynamic and innovative tech environment.
Responsibilities
  1. AI Development:
    • Contribute to AI projects under the guidance of experienced professionals.
    • Work on tasks related to machine learning, natural language processing, or computer vision.
  2. Collaboration:
    • Engage in collaborative AI research and development efforts with the Techolution team.
    • Participate in team discussions to share insights and ideas.
  3. Learning and Skill Development:
    • Gain practical experience with AI technologies and frameworks.
    • Expand your knowledge of AI algorithms, models, and applications.
  4. Problem Solving:
    • Tackle challenges in AI development and problem-solving to enhance your skills.
    • Apply AI concepts to real-world scenarios and projects.
Gains and Benefits
  • Certificates: Receive a certificate of completion at the end of the internship, recognizing your contributions and the skills gained during the program.
  • Letter of Recommendation: Obtain a personalized letter of recommendation based on your performance during the internship.
  • Performance-based Stipend: Enjoy a stipend of up to 6,000 rupees, with the potential for additional incentives based on exceptional performance during the 3-month internship period.
  • Real-world AI Experience: Work on actual AI projects, applying machine learning and AI concepts in a professional setting.
Qualifications
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, or a related field.
  • Strong interest and foundational knowledge in artificial intelligence and machine learning.
  • Familiarity with AI frameworks and tools is an advantage.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
  • Eagerness to learn and contribute to a fast-paced AI development environment.
Additional Information
  • This is a 3-month internship position with the possibility of extension based on performance and business needs.
  • The internship offers valuable insights into AI development and an opportunity to work with experienced professionals at Techolution.

If you are a motivated student eager to gain practical experience in artificial intelligence, Techolution invites you to apply. Join us for a dynamic learning experience and elevate your skills in the exciting world of AI. Apply now!
